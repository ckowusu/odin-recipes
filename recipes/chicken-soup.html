What Is Marry Me Chicken Soup?

Marry Me Chicken Soup is the favorite cozy recipe of creamy, tangy chicken in stew form with rich flavors of sun-dried tomato, onion, garlic, chicken, cheese, and more.

Chicken Soup Ingredients

  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Onion, garlic, and tomato paste round out the rest of the flavor base.
  • Chicken broth and heavy cream
  • Medium shell pasta
  • basil, red pepper flakes, spinach, cream cheese, and Parmesan.

How to Make Homemade Marry Me Chicken Soup

  1. Heat oil from sun-dried tomatoes in a large Dutch oven over medium until shimmering. Add onion and garlic; cook, stirring often, until softened, 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Add tomato paste and sun-dried tomatoes; cook, stirring constantly, until tomato paste is slightly deeper red in color, about 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in broth, cream, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and crushed red pepper; bring to a boil over medium, stirring occasionally, about 12 minutes.
  4. Add pasta and reduce heat to medium-low; cook, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente, about 12 minutes.
  5. CReduce heat to low, and add spinach, chicken, cream cheese, and Parmesan; cook, stirring often, until cream cheese and Parmesan are melted and chicken is heated through, about 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with additional Parmesan, basil, and red pepper. Serve immediately.
